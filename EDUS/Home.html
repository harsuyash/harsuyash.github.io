This is a template of the journey of a lecture that has been designed to organize your content for better engagement in online learning. Use these 4 steps to make your lectures much more engaging in just a few minutes

Teaser

To describe the topic, set clear learning goals and perhaps an interesting trivia to catch attention. This helps in getting students excited and anticipate.

Lecture + Discussions

Teachers can upload the lecture content that they have created here as a pdf. Add recommended interactive contents for in-between discussions.

Reflect

What were the takeaways from the lecture? What remains unsolved and unanswered? What were the most interesting and most difficult parts? Students reflect

Checkout

Get feedbacks from students. What goal could be set for next lectures? What more could they like? Students can checkout with some thought and feeling.
